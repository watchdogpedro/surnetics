Section 01

Home Page Revisions

Three targeted changes to the homepage — removing outdated badge text, correcting the flow animation direction, and updating the team bio for accuracy.

Change 1 of 3

Remove Patent Badge from Hero Section

The "US Patent 9,968,930 B2 · Active" badge pill that appeared above the headline in the hero was removed. The patent information is fully represented elsewhere on the site (IP Portfolio page, stats bar) and the badge added visual clutter to the hero without adding strategic value.

Change 2 of 3

Correct Flow Direction in Hero Animation

The microfluidic channel animation labels were swapped to accurately represent the physics. Fluid self-propels from a region of Low Surface Energy toward High Surface Energy — matching the mechanism described in the patent and the Technology page text.

Before (incorrect)

Left: HIGH SURFACE ENERGY → Right: LOW SURFACE ENERGY

After (corrected)

Left: LOW SURFACE ENERGY → Right: HIGH SURFACE ENERGY

Change 3 of 3

Update Brian Babcock Bio — Team Strip

Updated the credential line in the "Team Behind the Technology" strip at the bottom of the homepage.

Before

Registered US Patent Agent · Vanderbilt B.S./M.S. ChemE · Argonne National Laboratory

After

Microfluidics and Coatings Expert — Registered US Patent Agent · Vanderbilt B.S./M.S. ChemE

Section 02

Technology Page Revisions

Two corrections to the Technology page — the gradient diagram direction and the patent protection date.

Change 1 of 2

Correct Flow Direction in Channel Diagram

The gradient channel cross-section diagram on the Technology page had its energy labels swapped — aligned to match the hero animation and the correct underlying physics. Flow arrow direction (left → right) is unchanged; the labels now correctly read LOW ENERGY on the left and HIGH ENERGY on the right.

Change 2 of 2

Update Patent Protection Horizon — "The Only Commercial Source"

The protection date in the "The Only Commercial Source" section was updated to reflect the full portfolio protection window including continuation applications.
